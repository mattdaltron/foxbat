Started in the summer of 2015 by friends Clay and Joe. Robby, childhood friend of Joe, came in on drums. Jeromy had played with their previous bands before but had never met them. He happened to meet Clay and get into talks about music, which led to him joining. Chris, another long time friend of the other members, was asked to be a second guitar, finalizing the roster in the winter. Released the first EP, Eat My Young, July of 2016, through Little Heart Records. Finishing up the final bits for our first full length, Rotgut, to be released in the early summer, to be released through Eastwood Records.
