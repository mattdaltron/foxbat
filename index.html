Foxbat emerged from their furry cocoon in the summer of 2015. Friends Clay Nevels (vocals, formerly of I, Omega) and Joe Brock (guitar, formerly of Uh Huh, Baby Yeah!) formed the initial core of the band.

Robby Ward (drums), childhood friend of Joe, agreed to sit in on drums.

Jeromy Johns (bass) had played with their previous bands, but had never had the chance to meet any of them. A conversation about music at a bar lead to him joining.

Chris 'Biff' Hysell (guitar), another longtime friend, joined as a second guitar, finalizing the band's lineup.

In July of 2016 the band released their debut EP "Eat My Young" on Little Heart Records. Currently the fellas are finalizing their full length debut for Eastwood Records, "Rotgut".
